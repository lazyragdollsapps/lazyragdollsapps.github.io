Getting started

Three steps. Then you can't stop.

Instantly intuitive — the depth reveals itself naturally.

1

Pick a shape

Three block shapes sit in your tray. Drag any one of them onto the 8×8 grid to see where it will land.

2

Fill the grid

Place shapes to complete full rows and columns. Plan several moves ahead for maximum chain combos.

3

Watch it explode

Completed lines vanish in a burst of particles. Chain clears for escalating combos — the bigger the chain, the bigger the boom.

How scoring works

Every placement earns points. Clear lines to multiply them. Keep your combo alive to score big.

Score = (cells placed × 10) + floor(clearBonus × comboMultiplier × levelMultiplier)
Placement points — 10 × cells
Dot (1 cell)10 pts
2-cell shapes20 pts
3-cell shapes30 pts
4-cell shapes (L, T, S, line)40 pts
5-cell shapes (line, plus)50 pts
3×3 square (9 cells)90 pts
